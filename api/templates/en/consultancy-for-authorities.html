CONSULTANCY FOR CONTRACTING AUTHORITIES

We offer consultancy services in the field of public procurement and associated regulations to support contracting authorities in the procurement and management of public contracts. This includes advice, assistance, and expertise in various aspects such as the preparation of tender documents, evaluation of bids, negotiation and conclusion of contracts, monitoring and management of contracts during implementation, and dispute resolution.

Here are some key aspects related to consultancy for contracting authorities:

Compliance with regulations: Public procurement is subject to strict and complex rules and procedures aimed at ensuring transparency, competition, and efficient use of public funds. Consultants specialized in public procurement are familiar with the current legislation and regulations and can assist contracting authorities in complying with them in the procurement process.

Preparation of tender documents: Consultants can provide support in preparing and drafting tender documents such as terms of reference, technical specifications, evaluation criteria, and deadlines. These documents need to be clear, precise, and fair, ensuring the receipt of the best offers from suppliers.

Evaluation of bids: Consultants can assist contracting authorities in evaluating bids received in tender procedures. This involves analyzing and comparing bids based on the evaluation criteria established in the tender documents to select the supplier offering the best value for money and meeting the contracting authority's requirements.

Negotiation and contract conclusion: Consultants can provide assistance in the process of negotiating contracts with selected suppliers and ensure that the contractual terms and conditions are in line with the objectives and requirements of the contracting authority.

Monitoring and contract management: Consultants can support contracting authorities in monitoring and managing contracts during implementation. This may include monitoring supplier performance, administering contract changes, resolving disputes, and evaluating contract outcomes.

Consultancy for contracting authorities contributes to increasing the efficiency and effectiveness of public procurement, ensuring optimal use of resources, and respecting the fundamental principles of public procurement. Therefore, contracting authorities can benefit from the expertise and specialized knowledge of our consultants in this field.
